Current Main Project:

Defining metabolic bottlenecks in the CIC (Citrate-Isotrate Carrier, SLC25A1) regulatory system to increase cancer-specific radiosensitivity in normal oxygenation as well as severe hypoxic conditions.

We are using LC-MS system to get signal intensities of metabolites (>170) corresponding to concentration of each metabolite and normalize them to the cell quantity of the probe under each condition setup. We then perform multiple statistical analysis such as GSVA enrichment, pattern-based change, PCA to define metabolites most contributing to variance between conditions and many other approaches.

Apart from data analysis we also take our observations to experiment with causes of cell death such as radiation- and SLC25A1i-induced lipid peroxidation analysis and its correlation with Fe2+/Fe3+ proportion to define ferroptotic rates in D(R)-2-hydroxyglutarate or CTPI2-induced SLC25A1's activity inhibition conditions that lead to pruning cell system towards error-prone NHEJ pathway of DNA dsDamage Response.
